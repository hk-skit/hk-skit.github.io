A Walk to Coorg & Mysore

A couple of months ago I went to Bangulru for some project work. So during weeked I got chance to visit Coorg. Coorg is around 270km away from Bangluru. My friend Vikas was planning to go to Coorg. He insisted me to join. So I went with him. I am glad that I joined him because I will not find place like Coorg in north India.

Coorg has so many splendid things. It has hills, lakes, safaris, river rafting, milky clouds, cute coffe beans, black papper and delicious juicy wine and many more.
